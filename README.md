Crosswords
==========

Crosswords lets you play crosswords on Ubuntu.  It is based on Ubuntu's
[HTML5 SDK][1].

[1]: http://developer.ubuntu.com/api/html5/sdk-14.04/

Crosswords is in very early development.  Right now, there's no
installation procedure.  After downloading it, just run
`ubuntu-html5-app-launcher --www=www` from the directory.  Or just open
up `www/index.html` with your web browser.
